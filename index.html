Core Values of Granthgram:

Granthgram revolves around three core values : Individuality , confidence , and freedom of speech. These values are reflected in all the community’s activities

Individuality:

The community believes every person has a unique perspective, and it encourages members to embrace their distinctiveness. Granthgram provides a platform for personal growth and celebrates self-expression. Members are free to share their thoughts and ideas through debates, creative writing, or storytelling, helping them develop their unique voice.

Confidence:

Granthgram aims to build confidence by offering platforms like public speaking and debates. These activities help members articulate their thoughts assertively and grow more self-assured. The confidence gained through these experiences extends beyond the community and enhances members’ personal and professional lives.

Freedom of Speech:

The community champions open dialogue and unfiltered expression. In today’s polarized world, Granthgram provides a space for respectful discussions on diverse opinions, allowing members to express themselves without fear of censorship or judgment. This commitment to freedom of speech fosters intellectual curiosity and meaningful conversations on challenging topics.
